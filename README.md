# VO Contact Frontend

The HM Revenue and Customs Valuation Office Contact Frontend is a web-based service that enables users to submit enquiries to the Valuation Office regarding property-related matters such as Council Tax and business rates.

This service is part of the GOV.UK digital platform and provides a guided, structured way for users to contact the Valuation Office without needing to navigate multiple contact channels.

## Purpose of the Service

The application is designed to:

- Provide a digital contact channel for users with valuation-related queries
- Replace or reduce reliance on phone/email support by offering a self-service enquiry journey
- Route user enquiries efficiently to the correct team within the Valuation Office
- Improve user experience by guiding users through relevant questions before submission

The Valuation Office (now part of HMRC) is responsible for valuing properties for Council Tax and business rates, making it a key contact point for property-related taxation queries.

## What the website does

[The live service](https://www.qa.tax.service.gov.uk/valuation-office-agency-contact-frontend/new-or-existing-enquiry) provides a step-by-step form journey where users:

1. Select whether their enquiry is new or existing
2. Choose the type of enquiry (e.g. Council Tax band, business rates)
3. Provide details about the property or issue
4. Submit supporting information
5. Send the enquiry directly to the appropriate Valuation Office team

This structured flow ensures that:
- Users are asked only relevant questions
- Enquiries are complete and actionable
- Internal teams can process requests more efficiently
